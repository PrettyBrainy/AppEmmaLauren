Let's Learn! Information to Get Started

The average American home spends about $2,000 per year on energy bills alone. According to Energy Star, you can save $575, or 30% of that cost per year, if your home uses only Energy Star certified appliances. That’s the same as saving 5,500 pounds of greenhouse gas emissions every year!


Since 2005, Energy Star’s efforts have resulted in a 9% reduction in the total carbon emissions for the United States, a savings on energy bills of more than $362 billion, and the prevention of the emission of 2 billion tons of greenhouse gas emissions. Check out this link to find out what makes a product energy star certified!
