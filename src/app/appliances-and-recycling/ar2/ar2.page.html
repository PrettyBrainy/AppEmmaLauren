Peace Out to the Personal Printer

Let's Learn! Information to Get Started

Did you know that in the U.S. 8 ink cartridges are thrown away per second and up to 691,200 are thrown away per day? These ink and toner cartridges end up in landfills and release volatile organic compounds and heavy metals into the soil and water, polluting our environment. It can take 1,000 years for these cartridges to decompose in a landfill! Cartridges you throw away today will still be on Earth in the year 3020.


The manufacturing of these cartridges also takes a toll on our environment. To make just one cartridge, about 4.8 kg of carbon dioxide are emitted into the environment and, when you factor in the manufacturing of the toner, that number jumps to 8 kg of carbon dioxide! That’s like burning 9 pounds of coal.

Take the Pledge Peace Out to the Personal Printer

Lead your family in an open conversation about recycling ink cartridges and donating or recycling personal printers. If you have a personal printer that is not necessary, donate it. Check your local Office Depot or Staples to see if they will recycle your ink and toner cartridges. Printing from personal printers can be replaced with printing from your local library/school.

Where to Recycle?

When recycling your ink cartridges and personal printer--if this applies to you, make sure to do it responsibly. Go to Earth911 to find out where you can safely recycle your ink cartridges and printer.
