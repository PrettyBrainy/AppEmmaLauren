Monitor the Monitor

Let's Learn! Information to Get Started

One personal computer left on overnight wastes enough electricity to print more than 500 pages AND enough carbon dioxide to power a double-decker bus! By shutting down the monitor on your computer, you can save up to 66% of the energy that a computer consumes.

Take the Pledge Monitor the Monitor

Save energy by turning off the monitor on your PC or your family’s shared computer. Put a sticky note on your computer reminding you to turn off your monitor when you walk away! Lead your friends and family in making these shut-down reminders for their own monitors and laptop computers.


A good rule is that if you’re going to be away from your computer for more than 20 minutes, shut down your monitor or laptop.
