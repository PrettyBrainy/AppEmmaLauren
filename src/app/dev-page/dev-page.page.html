Heidi A. Olinger

Angler & CEO (Chief Experience Officer)

CONTENT EDITOR

“It does not require many words to speak the truth.” — Chief Joseph of the Nez Perce (1840-1904)

I am a writer and the founder of Pretty Brainy, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit that gives girls and women the framework to build professional skills parallel to STEAM skills.

From hard-earned experience I know that, by combining clear communication and steady hustle, great things can be forged from nothing.

I am the author of Leonardo’s Science Workshop (Quarto 2019) and the co-founder, with Pretty Brainy’s student leaders, of MISSion Innovation™, an innovation marathon for climate action and a mobile app for helping to curb global warming by leading a carbon-conscious life. One of my favorite days, ever, was delivering the keynote at Hewlett-Packard’s Tech Camp, a gathering of 100 sixth-grade girls learning how to create technology.

I was honored by my peers as the 2017 Woman of Inspiration, an award presented by Colorado Women of Influence, and I have been a member of the cohort at the Harvard Graduate School of Education Future of Learning Institute at Project Zero.

I hold a journalism bachelor of arts, with an emphasis in science and technology journalism, from the University of Wisconsin-Madison, and a master of arts in English literature and language, with an emphasis in 19th-century American studies and the law, from the University of Colorado at Boulder.

I frame each day by practicing transcendental meditation and am accompanied everywhere by my little dog, Patches.
