Let's Learn! Information to Get Started

Throughout the previous transportation pledges, you have learned that taking public transit is an efficient, cheap, and environmentally conscious form of travel. To make your trip on public transportation successful, however, you may need to prepare yourself for traveling. For Northern Colorado, use our region’s public transportation websites, linked below, to learn exact dates, times, locations, and costs of your ride. If you prepare in advance and stay safe while riding, it will be a wonderful tool to save money and our environment.

Take the Pledge Navigator, Not Carbon Traitor!

With your family, explore the options in your region for public transportation. Sit down and plan your route far in advance, making sure you know bus fare, bus stop locations, and times in which you need to get on the bus. To keep yourself safe, try to have the exact amount of money for your fare so you aren’t seen with excess cash, keep your personal belongings on your lap, and stay alert while riding. You may be able to ride for free: see the websites above for details.


If possible, sit in an aisle seat to avoid being boxed in and sit toward the front of the bus near the driver. Once you have a solid plan and have gone over how to stay safe while riding public transportation, put it into action and try out your route with your family!
