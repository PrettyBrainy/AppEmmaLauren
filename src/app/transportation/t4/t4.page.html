Time For Online

Let's Learn! Information to Get Started

When we reduce the number of in-person events, activities, and meetings we attend, we reduce our carbon footprint. Though virtual happenings may seem difficult and less productive than in-person events, they can be successful while simultaneously saving the environment. Here are tips to make your video events a success:


1. Before you begin, check out how you will look on camera: review how your face is framed, if the light in the room is casting your face in too much dark or light, and how your surroundings add to or take away from your look. What are people going to see when they see you online? The settings tab of your online platform may have some instant enhancements for lighting and color tone. Otherwise, make time for adjustments!


2. Turn on your camera: to make the meeting seem personal and appealing, turn on your video. Facial expressions are important to a successful connection because they can show, for example, someone who isn’t paying attention, someone who is exasperated, or someone who is having a great experience!


3. Make an agenda: to make your events effective and to avoid awkward silences and wasted time, make a plan, listing most to least important items. This will keep you focused on what’s key and lead to greater conversations.


4. Connect people: Make sure to ask everyone how they are doing and what is going on with their lives. This will help put people at ease and make them more comfortable sharing in the meeting. Remind everyone to respect one another by not interrupting.


5. Don’t mute and don’t multitask: ask everyone to unmute themselves and stay engaged. In order to have the best experience, everyone should be involved and engaged. Anyone who is looking at a seperate tab or checking email or social won’t be as committed to bringing their all to the experience.


6. Give each person time to talk: make sure everyone has the chance to contribute. Ask another person to keep the virtual gathering on track: having someone else make sure everyone is respectful and focused will help the event run smoothly.
