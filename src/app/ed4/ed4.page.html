Take the Pledge Haven't You Heard, Spread the Word Here are the guidelines for becoming a Cool Climate Ambassador:

  1. Team Up

  2. Pledge to use the MISSion Innovation App for a full 7 weeks.

  3. Get at least three others to sign up for the MISSion Innovation App.

  4. Do at least one thing to help encourage climate action in your community, such as lowering your computer brightness by 50%, picking up trash from public areas, or throwing your plastic bottles in a recycling bin.


Then, share it with us. By sharing your action you’ll verify your new action and win points. Tell us what you did and upload an image! Complete all items on the list and you will become an official Cool Climate Ambassador for your community.
